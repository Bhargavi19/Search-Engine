Information of Statue of Unity Project - World’s Tallest Monuments

Statue of Unity Facts

DidYouKnow the StatueofUnity will have a viewing gallery at 153 m, which can accommodate up to 200 visitors at a go, and will offer an expansive view of the dam and environs? WorldsTallestStatue

DidYouKnow the time for construction of the StatueofUnity, the WorldsTallestStatue, is fixed at 42 months? No escalation on labour, fuel, and material is allowed.

DidYouKnow the StatueofUnity, the WorldsTallestStatue, will be able to withstand wind velocity up to 60 m/sec, vibration and earthquake? — at Sardar Sarovar Dam.

DidYouKnow 22500Mton of cement will be used to build the StatueofUnity, the WorldsTallestStatue?

The Narmada, the fifth longest river in the Indian subcontinent, runs in a rift valley between the Vindhya and Satpura ranges. The Sardar Sarovar Dam and its 200 Km long reservoir, Satpura and Vindhya mountain ranges from the gallery of the StatueofUnity WorldsTallestStatue

DidYouKnow that 5,700 Mton of structural steel, and 18,500 Mton of reinforcement bars will be used to build the StatueofUnity, theWorldsTallestStatue?

The StatueofUnity is being built on Sadhu Island in the Narmada river, approximately 3.2 km away from the Sardar Sarovar Dam. DidYouKnow a 3.5 km highway will be built to connect the island to Kevadia town in Gujarat? WorldsTallestStatue

Building Bonds of Iron -- DidYouKnow that the iron collected through the 'Loha' campaign will be melted, converted into rebar, and used in the foundation of the StatueofUnity, making farmers across India an integral part of building the WorldsTallestStatue?

DidYouKnow that after completion, the StatueofUnity will generate approximately 15,000 direct jobs for tribal people every year?WorldsTallestStatue

DidYouKnow? The Shrestha Bharat Bhavan adjacent to the Statue of Unity is designed to be a 128-key, 3-star hotel facility with food service, guest amenities, and conference facilities. WorldsTallestStatue

DidYouKnow? The Statue of Unity will comprise two semi-joined, composite concrete cylindrical cores, surrounded by a structural steel space frame to support the exterior cladding. WorldsTallestStatue
